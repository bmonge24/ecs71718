Click on the button below to learn the feeding habits of a German Shepard!

Due to German Shepards energetic lifestyle, they require a power packed diet. They are carnivores meaning they require a higher amount of protein in their diet. It is recomended that your German Shepard gets at least 22% protein in their diet in order to keep them strong, healthy, and to allow proper growth. Fat is also important in their diet because it gives taste to its food, howver too much isn't healthy but too little may cause skin problems. Fat is recomended to be 5%-8% of their diet. When it comes to German Shepard puppies, they normally eat more than the adults because lots of this food contribiutes to your puppies growth.
